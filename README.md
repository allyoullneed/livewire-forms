# All you'll need - Livewire forms for Statamic

Render your forms in 1 line of code, with powerful components and a Livewire-powered backend.

## From Blueprints directly into the front end

Render your form in 1 line:
```html
<livewire:statamic-form in="contact_form" />
```
And you'll get a form whose layout follows its blueprint.
<img width="1485" height="492" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/54e02ef5-03ab-4d60-a248-b27703d91dac" />

## Supports Livewire

Leverage the power of Livewire for input validation and submission.
Your forms won't load a new page upon submission anymore.
